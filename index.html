About me.

My name is Emma Warshofsky. I am a student at the University of Michigan. I love photography, so this portfolio includes some photographs I have taken throughout the past few years.

I am a junior from Westchester, New York studying UX design. Some of my hobbies include seeing live music, spending time with friends, traveling, trying new restaurants, and watching Michigan sports. This website will hopefully show you some of this through the photos I have taken!
